The Weeknd

by Maia Botek

With four Grammys, billions of streams and countless awards, Abel Tesfaye has skyrocketed to success over the course of his 10+ year career.

Better known as The Weeknd, the artist and producer has amassed fans for his music that taps into genres from R&B to funk and pop, exploring themes like heartbreak, love and addiction.
